Privacy Policy

Musiqly is designed to respect your privacy. This page explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it is handled. The short version: your music stays on your device and we do not sell or share your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Musiqly is a music player and live radio application for Android, developed and maintained independently. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at riva24x@outlook.com.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Musiqly does not:

  • Create user accounts or require registration
  • Collect, transmit, or store personal data on any server we operate
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs that track individual users
  • Display advertising or share data with advertisers
  • Sell, rent, or trade any information about you to third parties

3. Device Permissions

Musiqly requests the following Android permissions and uses them solely for the stated purpose:

READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Required to scan your device storage for audio files and build your local music library. Files are read locally — no audio content is uploaded anywhere.

INTERNET
Used for two optional features only:

  • Live radio — streams audio from publicly available radio station URLs that you select.
  • Album artwork — if enabled, queries Apple's public iTunes Search API to fetch artwork for tracks that are missing it. Only the artist and album name are sent as search terms in a standard HTTPS request; no personal data is included.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK
Keeps music playing while the app is in the background and powers the lock-screen media notification.

WAKE_LOCK
Prevents the CPU from sleeping during playback so audio does not cut out.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Allows the home-screen widget (if used) to restore its state after a device restart.

VIBRATE
Used only for haptic feedback on button taps within the app; not used to send any data.

4. Local Data Storage

All app data — your library, playlists, playback history, settings, and cached artwork — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database (WatermelonDB) and the device file system. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a playlist file (M3U) using the app's share feature.

Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Last.fm (optional)
If you choose to connect a Last.fm account in Settings, Musiqly will send the title, artist, album, and duration of each track you play to Last.fm for scrobbling. This integration is entirely opt-in and can be disconnected at any time. Last.fm's own Privacy Policy governs how they handle that data.

iTunes Search API (optional)
When automatic artwork fetching is enabled, Musiqly queries Apple's public iTunes Search API using only the artist and album name of a track that has no artwork. No account, login, or identifier is sent. Apple's Privacy Policy governs Apple's handling of those requests.

Radio station streams
When you play a radio station, your device connects directly to that station's streaming server. The station provider may log your IP address as part of normal server operation. Musiqly has no control over those logs.

6. Children's Privacy

Musiqly is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Security

Because Musiqly does not transmit your personal data to any server we operate, the risk of a data breach affecting your information is minimal. All network requests the app makes (radio streams, iTunes API, Last.fm if enabled) are made over HTTPS.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users via an in-app notice. Continued use of Musiqly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Musiqly's data practices, please reach out:

Email: riva24x@outlook.com

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
