What's inside

Everything you need
in one app

Musiqly brings your local music collection and live radio together with a clean, distraction-free interface.

🎵

Local Music Library

Automatically scans your device for audio files. Browse by song, album, artist, or genre — all beautifully organised.

📋

Smart Playlists

Favourites, Recently Added, Most Played, and Not Played playlists update automatically. Create your own custom playlists too.

📻

Live Radio

Stream thousands of radio stations from around the world. Search by name, genre, or country and save your favourites.

🎨

Beautiful Now Playing

Dynamic album art with vinyl disc animation, adaptive accent colours pulled from your artwork, and a built-in lyrics viewer.

🎛️

Equalizer

Fine-tune your sound with a built-in equalizer. Presets for every genre and full manual control for audiophiles.

🔀

Queue & Playback Controls

Full queue management, shuffle, three repeat modes, crossfade, gapless playback, and lock-screen controls.

🖼️

Artwork Fetching

Missing album art? Musiqly fetches it automatically from the internet and caches it locally on your device.

🌙

Dark Theme & Customisation

Stunning dark UI with customisable accent colours. The player adapts its colour scheme to your currently playing album art.
